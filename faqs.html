Frequently Asked Questions

How many kitties are there?

There are so many kitties for you to play with! In accordance with North Carolina Law, we can have up to 12 kitties at a time. All cats are spayed, neutered, fully vaccinated, and microchipped as well.

Can I bring my cat from home?

While we love all cats, out of courtesy for our cats and yours, please leave your cat at home! We have plenty that will want your undivided attention.

Do I need a reservation?

Yes! We are often completely booked. Walk-ins are allowed when space is available, but that is extremely rare. We encourage reservations above anything so that we can make sure we don't disappoint you. Weekends, especially, can be booked as much as 3-4 weeks out, so please plan accordingly!

What can I expect when entering the Cat Lounge?

Cats are everywhere! If you love cats, it'll definitely be a fun experience for you. The Cat Lounge is 2 stories, and with 12 cats around, there will always be someone to play with. However, we ask that you be respectful and do not wake any sleeping kitties, and picking up kitties is prohibited. The best advice we can give is to grab a toy and a comfy spot and see who comes to you!

Is there an age policy?

Out of respect for other guests and the safety of both the cats and little ones, children under 6 years of age can only be allowed in the Cat Lounge when the room is booked out by the entire family. To book an entire room, a total of 8 individual spots must be filled. Please ensure you have booked these sessions appropriately out of courtesy for our other visitors.

For children aged 7–13, a waiver must be signed by an adult and that adult must accompany them at all times while they are at the Cat Lounge. For those aged 14–17, a waiver is still needed, but they are allowed in the Cat Lounge without supervision.

I met the cat for me. Now what?

That's amazing news and exactly what we're here for. Please go to our rescue sight Adoption Page and fill out the application. They will contact you to discuss all the details and guide you through the process of bringing a new cat into your life!

Are we hiring?

Currently, we are not hiring. But we always welcome resumes and CVs for future hires, so please reach out to info@cattalescatcafe.com to send in any applications!

Have More Questions?

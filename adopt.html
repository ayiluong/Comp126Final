Adoption!

Let's Get the Yarn Ball Moving...

You can apply for a specific cat OR submit a general application and let our cat matchmakers guide you to the perfect friend. We have an easy and QUICK adoption application. After submitting an application, you will be contacted by email to answer questions, make suggestions, and connect you with your dream cat (or cats!). Click the green button below for more detailed information about the adoption process.

Cat Tales Rescue NC has no application fee. The adoption cost is $150 or two for $250. 100% of the adoption cost goes back to the rescue to help save the next cat. All kitties at Cat Tales Cat Cafe are fully adoption ready, including spay/neuter, vaccinated, microchipped, tested for FIV/FeLV and have had a wellness vet check. They are ready to roll!

We LOVE getting adoption updates! Check out the hashtags #cattalescatcafe and #cattalesnextchapter and add your chapter to our Cat Tales story!

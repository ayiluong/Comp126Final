About Us

Mission

Here at Cat Tales Cafe we provide a low-stress environment for kitties looking for a new home. You can come cuddle up to a new friend, have a drink (wine, beer, and coffee are all served), and help to socialize the cats so that they're ready for their forever homes. We currently are home to 12 terrific kitties, and if you fall in love with any of them, you can adopt your soul -cat!

The Cats

Meet our Crew!

Cat Tales Cat Cafe is essentially a large foster home for cats rescued by our nonprofit sister business, Cat Tales Rescue NC. In addition to the kitties at the cafe, we have many awesome cats available in foster homes. These kitties are awaiting their turn at the cat cafe but can be adopted straight from their foster homes. If there is a particular kitty that catches your eye, please reach out to confirm their location before making a reservation at the cat cafe.
